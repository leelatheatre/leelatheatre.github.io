MONOLOGUE (solo acting)

It is an acting competition for solo performers. Participant has to record a video of their performance and send it to us.

  • All participants must be between 15 years to 24 years of age.
  • All information of the participant must be provided on the entry form.
  • The duration of the acting video should be 2 minutes (give or take 10 seconds).
  • The file size should be between 50-60 MB.
  • The name of the file to be submitted should be in this format- "Participant’s Name_Title of the performnce". Eg. Vraj Gupta_Zindagi
  • All entries must be sent by mail to leelatheatre@gmail.com.
  • The subject of the mail should be- PANGA | MONOLOGUE
  • The entry fee is Rs. 300/- each participant and is non-refundable.
  • Entry fee can be paid to the following UPI I'd- rastogi.akansh@okhdfcbank
  • Take a screenshot of the 'transaction successful' message. This has to be attached in the form.
  • Only one submission per participant is allowed for each category.
