Harshika Pareek

A question which is always asked to me “What am I doing in theatre???”, as professionally I belong to psychology field. Answer is I am not learning how to act but with the guidance of Raju sir and being in Leela “The Magical Theatre Group” I have and I am still developing the qualities that are required to be a good psychologist and a counsellor. In a jot being a patient listener, resorting clashes, self-awareness, self-confidence, self-esteem, channelizing interaction, empathy, positive regard, cooperation, coordination and list goes on. As theatre is not just acting on stage but it is a dish that is garnished with team work and what makes it beautiful & tasty is proper communication and organisation. And also being the Creative Head of Leela, as an add-on topping I have improved and excelled my writing skills, presentation strength and above all, qualities of creative genre.
