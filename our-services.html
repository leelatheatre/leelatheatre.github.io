Our Achievements

Workshop

Leela “The Magical Theatre” group organizes different types of theatre workshops of suitable durations ranging from a week to a month. These workshops are organized in schools, colleges, and different renowned Institutions, which inculcates theatrical activities and focuses on the preparation and performance of a street or a stage play at the end of it.

Consult

Leela is a hub of young and creating potentials who are experts in their respective fields. They are Graphic designers, engineers, entrepreneurs, choreographers, fashion designers, pyschologists, photographer, computer experts, writers, editors, actors, etc. So, as a round the clock service leela provides an opportunity to seek professional consultany from its team of members.

School Annual Productions

School Annual Productions is also one of the important venture of Leela, where the team deals with the organization of school annual functions on a grand scale. These school annual productions are overall musical theatrical shows prepared with the school students. Leela is been known and appreciated for these productions in different prestigious schools all over India

Personality Development Classes

Another interesting activity of “Leela”- personality development is an important sphere in which team works to bring out the treasure of hidden potential of the individual participants. The main focuses are the development of the communication skills, interactive capabilities, self-confidence, leadership qualities, self-expression, team building, and element of cooperation and coordination with the recognition of assets of the young adults. Above all building of street smartness i.e. to face and deal with sudden and situational demands of life.

Theatre in Education Programme

One of the famous and nationally renowned programmes of “Leela” is ‘Theatre in Education’. It deals with the conversion of school subject books into theatre and making learning of syllabus easier by expressing it through theatrical performances. Leela has converted a lot many books of commerce i.e. Partnership in Peace, Accountancy in Life, Business Studies, and many more into field of theatre. And these productions were highly appreciated by prominent personalities like Leela Samson (Renowned Bharatnatyam Dancer), Berry Jones (Expert in Theatre), etc. And taking one step ahead ‘Leela’ is now working on conversion of books of management, engineering, journalism and psychology.
